About Us

DEEMED TO BE UNIVERSITY

The Gandhi Institute of Technology and Management, commonly known as GITAM, Visakhapatnam is one of the best private engineering Deemed to be University established in 1980. It was previously known as GITAM College. GITAM has been approved by UGC. It has been granted the status of deemed to be university in 2007. GITAM aims to impart futuristic and comprehensive education of global standards and is responsible for imparting education in the field of Engineering and Technology.
